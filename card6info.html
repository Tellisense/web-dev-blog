Starting Your Project: The Right Way

So, you've decided to begin a new web development project. But you know that you are going to mess something up at the very beginning causing your instructor to loose his mind. That is why you have come here; to ensure that that doesn't happen.

So before you even begin to make any project, it is highly recommended that you create a designated web development folder where you will place all future projects. Once you have done this you are now ready to create a new folder on your desktop. Name the folder as something relevant to your project then drag it into your web-dev-folder
